## Visual Communication
* **[NET ZERO TARGET Video Campaign:](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CkLj5TfnHtg)** - This campaign addresses Türkiye's vision for smart urban transformation within the context of its commitment to the 2053 Net Zero Emissions Target and the Paris Agreement. It also highlights the significant share of cities in global energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions, emphasizing the necessity of transitioning from fossil fuels to renewable energy to achieve a sustainable future. The model proposed by SÜGEP Young Sustainability Leaders leverages urban transformation as an opportunity, calling for a legal regulation mandating the construction of self-generating buildings equipped with photovoltaic systems. This model offers significant financial advantages to family budgets by eliminating electricity, heating, and vehicle fuel costs, while also achieving national and environmental goals by reducing energy dependence and carbon emissions.

* **[A project by high school students on buildings that generate their own energy has been submitted to the Turkish Parliament.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hNGTagvkve4)** - The SÜGEP leaders, a group of high school students from Izmir, have developed a pioneering project that turns urban regeneration into an opportunity to tackle the climate crisis. The model, which mandates the installation of photovoltaic systems that convert solar energy into electricity in new buildings, aims not only to contribute to the targets of the Paris Climate Agreement but also to recoup the investment costs within 38 months, thereby eliminating households’ three major bills: electricity, natural gas and even vehicle fuel.
